I'm Bad at Music Theory

Code Project

A chord generator and music theory aid for producers and songwriters who work intuitively — built on the premise that you don't need to know theory to use it, but using it might teach you some.

What It Does

  • Generate chord progressions in any key and mode
  • Explore chord voicings and inversions visually
  • Suggest next chords based on common voice leading and progressions
  • Label what you're doing (optionally) so you learn by doing, not by studying
  • Export to MIDI or notation formats

Philosophy

Most music theory tools are for people who already know music theory. This is for the person who writes by ear, wants to know why something sounds good, and doesn't have 200 hours to spend on textbooks.
