Xhungus

Code Project

A tool for converting a personal Twitter/X archive into structured Obsidian notes — preserving years of posts, threads, and social writing as a navigable knowledge graph.

What It Does

Takes the raw data export from Twitter/X and transforms it into a format compatible with the Second Brian personal archive system — turning ephemeral social media posts into durable, linked notes.

  • Parses Twitter/X archive export format
  • Converts tweets and threads into Obsidian-compatible markdown
  • Preserves timestamps, metadata, and reply context
  • Integrates with the Second Brian rhizomatic note structure

Stack

Python Obsidian Markdown Archive Processing

Context

Part of a larger effort to own personal digital history — extracting meaning from a platform that may not always be accessible, and turning it into something that lasts.
