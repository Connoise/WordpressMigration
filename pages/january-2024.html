Poem

Recap on Genesis Lore

Endless body saturated with potential, structure dream precipitate on the sharpest point. Pressure condenses, develops equally in all directions. A crystalline bubble of consequence grows. 

Undulating consequence upon itself, a soapy film of connections : Universe. Simple rules and fundamental functions spill from an un-seeable primordial point. Each push inwards expressed an exponential stretch of the membrane.

Birthed consequence of consequence of dreams, pretend to be lost. Observe to live and reject the surface. Will the ocean invert a paradigm of oil? Will connections and rules sublimate to un-consequence? 

We will pretend to be surprised.

the Dunning-Kreuger curve is jank. The y-axis should be inverted as a bowl. Unwillingness to unlearn: Kunning-Dreuger. POV: the coaster car suspended on the precipice of action. A humming weakspot at the inflection blinks from the abyss. A confrontation with a reason to be proven wrong.
I don’t like to discuss what I learn. It feels from my voice like projection. Ego sludge coats me in a perfect mirrored film. We all have access to the wiki-hippocampus so speaking is like saying the joke but louder.
I want to continue to radiate pure quirky frequency, but I struggle to fill



The end state of millennial doomerism is to learn to love finding things to love. In 2023 I learned to love washing dishes.

In 2023 I started a medication combo that dissolved all of my thoughts. It carved channels through soft sulci. Topographies of maximum absolute production. Living felt like a natural spring hooked up to pressurized pipes. Even when I closed my mouth water escaped through the cracks.

I could only think one word at a time

When I first started, every morning, medication in hand, I would try to remove the dirty chemical smell of new* bupropion with a single breath. ppppffffff-. I got used to the taste but I still do it. Just to greet them. my nakama. Our secret handshake. The pressure is lower. The decayed grooves of production remain. I drip my contents and can hold no definite volume

* They dont make em like they used to


I had what I think is my first panic attack, which manifested as a plunge into dizziness and constant yawning. I actually just have a new 'thing' where I'm constantly yawning. Each yawn kinda feels like death crunching an ice cube while leaning 10degrees too far back in a flimsy desk chair. Its like the vacuum that follows a lightning bolt, from my face to my stomach. My jaw shivers and i bite my tongue to dampen the aftershock. I also enjoy cleaning now. I get by with less sleep. I suffer erratic micro-spasms and jerky muscle flexes. Water tastes bad. I go to work to create and come home to rest. Fitter, Happier–

It's a common thing with mental illness but I miss the bad times. Feeling rejected is a pit with no floor, but when you look up and see the surface shrink to a brilliant point you're suddenly just floating there. I was free to reject any or all parts of postmodern life. I lived through the seasons of bliss and suffering and nae-nae’d[gritty’d] over the quartz rumble of production. Hadal creatures, Dickinsonia-san, Sister Siphonophore, we understood the value of the cells in a leaf, an umbrella in the center of a street. Everything was beautiful and tragic and alive and already dead and I learned to love everything and I am no longer afraid of death.

In the absence of the sun the grain still bows its chaff to the rain : infinite compassion.

And now I am scared of sharing myself. I'm losing the momentum of madness. I have thoroughly become a crumbling stoic monument of inaction, observation.

I’m tired of post anxiety. The Realname vs Anon dichotomy is wearing the avatars of both apollo and dionysus. I want to post cringe. It’s the dirty living soul of the internet. But the old social spaces are no longer alive. The remaining theaters left to express the living soul only house the tools of diligence and dignity.

Every space is archived to be effortlessly interpreted and scored on a swealthy(wealthy sweaty?) Andy’s big data side hustle. Most communities dissolve before they even get started. Everyone is scared that the digital skin they touch is actually the lining of the stomach. Discord servers with discord servers to secretly talk about other discord servers. Nebula without stars.

so then there's

this-

I want to share things here. Like my medicines and my learned loves and my future plans. Monthly packets of myself. I’ll share song progress and other art. I want to precipitate my thoughts somewhere and I am paying 170+$ to host this site every year. To someone, maybe, the spill of my calcified innards will ring like a struck stalactite. 

Meeting the primordial source is an exchange to meet the last thing that can be loved. We already know, finding it will be sad. But the ocean expanded vast, deep, saturated with bliss. I want to return back to a life in the shallows. 

Thank you for reading, thank you for expanding the universe with your presence
